Beauty Tips & Trends

Skincare Routine: Morning vs Evening
Your morning routine should focus on protection (sunscreen, antioxidants) while evening is for repair and renewal (retinol, heavier moisturizers).
How to Choose Your Signature Scent
Test perfumes on your skin, not paper. Your body chemistry affects how a fragrance smells. Try it for a full day before deciding.
Makeup Tips for Kenya's Climate
In our warm climate, use lightweight, long-wearing formulas. Set with powder and use a setting spray for all-day wear.
Natural Beauty Trends 2026
The "no-makeup makeup" look continues to dominate - think dewy skin, soft brows, and sheer lip colors that enhance rather than cover.
